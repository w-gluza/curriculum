Study Abroad

Go Abroad

Internship | Erasmus | Exchange

As a full degree KEA student, you have the opportunity to study abroad for a semester at one of our international partner institutions. You can go abroad in either 3rd or 5th semester. It is also possible to spend the 6th semester (internship) abroad and to work in a foreign company. KEA has agreements with bests schools from Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Korea and more.

Outgoing Exchange

international academic experience

As a full degree KEA student, you have the opportunity to study for a semester at one of our international partner institutions. You can study one semester abroad without paying a fee at the foreign university. To apply for a Study Exchange you need to contact the International Coordinator for your field of study.

Erasmus Internship

international corporate experience

Erasmus Internship programme is available for any person studying a full degree in Europe and who is looking to conduct an internship in another European country. As an EU country citizen you are able to apply for an Erasmus grant. An Erasmus grant is scholarship program for all students enrolled in approved European universities.
