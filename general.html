Overview

of Multimedia Design and Communication

The goal of the Multimedia Design and Communication programme is to provide you with the skills needed to independently design, plan, realise and manage multimedia tasks and to manage multimedia productions.

Knowledge

understanding interdisciplinary issues

You have acquired knowledge of practice, theory and method relevant to analysis, ideation, design, planning, realisation and management of multimedia tasks as well as the implementation, administration and maintenance of multimedia productions and understanding of interdisciplinary multimedia issues in relation to both individual and team-based project work.

Skills

solving communication problems

You have gained the skills needed to realise and manage multimedia tasks and evaluate practice-oriented issues in the multimedia field. You can propose and select possible solutions, communicate practice-oriented issues and possible solutions to partners and users.

Competencies

participating in cooperation

You are able to handle development-oriented situations and be innovative in the tailoring of multimedia solutions to commercial conditions. You can engage in management and cooperation with others regardless of educational, linguistic and cultural background, participate cooperation and communication in networks based on a professional approach.
